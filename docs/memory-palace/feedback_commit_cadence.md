ezyang interjects "did you commit your stuff?" often. Commit
proactively at natural breakpoints — don't wait to be asked.

**Why:** he Ctrl-Cs often (uncommitted work is at risk) and reads
commit logs as status reports.

**How to apply:** after each coherent unit (fix, refactor pass,
feature step), commit. Match repo style: short imperative title,
optional one-line body — no PR-style paragraphs. Prefer too-small
splits over too-large.
